## 1. Overview

This bot ("ICA Mod Bot") is a Reddit Devvit application that automatically generates AI-powered summaries of Reddit posts once they reach a defined comment threshold. It is operated by the moderators of the subreddit(s) where it is installed.

## 3. What the App Does

- Monitors posts for comment activity
- When a post reaches the configured comment threshold, it fetches the top comments and sends them to the Google Gemini API to generate a summary
- Posts the summary as a distinguished moderator comment on the thread
